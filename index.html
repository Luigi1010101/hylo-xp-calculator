Hylo XP → Airdrop Estimator

Rough calculator for potential Hylo airdrop value based on XP.

Assumes a fixed total of 5,000,000,000 XP (5B), same as in @FabianoSolana's example.

Enter your XP, FDV and airdrop % and click “Estimate”.
Formula used:
airdrop pool = FDV × (airdrop% / 100)
value per XP = airdrop pool ÷ 5,000,000,000
your airdrop ≈ your XP × value per XP

This is a rough, purely speculative model based on numbers discussed by @FabianoSolana. Real airdrop mechanics, FDV and allocations can be very different (or zero). Tool put together by @Luigi1010101.
